Figure 2. Evidence Convergence Map: Natural Interventions Targeting the NRF2/Barrier Axis in Atopic Dermatitis
Legend — Evidence Strength Classification
Strong — Phase 3 RCTs, large meta-analyses, high-impact mechanistic studies (Nature), or validated omics
Moderate — In vitro cell biology, animal models, or prospective cohorts with moderate sample size
Preliminary — Pilot cohorts, small-n observational data, or hypothesis-generating studies
Each card shows the discipline, key quantitative finding, study citation with PubMed ID, study type, and evidence strength. Dashed lines represent mechanistic convergence on the central NRF2/barrier restoration pathway. Card header colors distinguish scientific disciplines; evidence-strength badges (lower-left of each card) use the three-tier scheme above.
