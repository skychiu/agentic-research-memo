🏆 Verdict: AIA Privilege Ultra is the better choice

For a 35-year-old woman planning to start a family, the AIA Privilege Ultra is the clear winner, despite being somewhat more expensive.

1. Pregnancy Complications Coverage Is the Dealbreaker

AIA covers pregnancy complications (ectopic pregnancy, pre-eclampsia, placenta praevia, HELLP syndrome, emergency C-section, etc.) in full after a 300-day waiting period. AXA excludes all maternity conditions and their complications entirely. Given that roughly 8–15% of pregnancies involve some form of complication, this single benefit alone can save tens or even hundreds of thousands of HKD.

2. Broader Benefits Out-of-the-Box

AIA includes TCM outpatient visits, Phase 3 clinical trial drugs, emergency dental, and private nursing on all plan tiers — not just the top tier.

3. Longer Post-Surgery Follow-Up

AIA allows 365 days of follow-up outpatient care after major surgery (vs. 180 days for AXA).

4. Lifetime Guaranteed Renewal

AIA's renewal is guaranteed for life with no age cap, versus AXA's age-100 limit.

Where AXA Wins

AXA offers no lifetime benefit cap, lower base premiums, more generous discount programmes (up to 35%), and immediate coverage of unknown pre-existing conditions. If pregnancy were not a factor, AXA would be highly competitive.

💡 Action Plan

  1. Apply for AIA Privilege Ultra now — the 300-day pregnancy complications waiting period starts on the policy effective date, so time is important.
  2. Choose the Asia geographic cover unless you plan to deliver overseas (outside Asia).
  3. Consider the HKD 25,000 deductible option (HKD 7,664/year) as a good balance between premium savings and out-of-pocket exposure.
  4. Join AIA Vitality for an instant 10% premium discount in Year 1.
  5. Budget at least HKD 7,000–19,000/year depending on your deductible choice.

🏆 結論：AIA 優越保 Ultra 是更佳選擇

對於一位35歲、計劃成家的女性，AIA 優越保 Ultra 是明顯較佳的選擇，儘管保費略高。

1. 懷孕併發症保障是決定性因素

AIA 在300天等候期後，全額保障懷孕併發症（宮外孕、子癇前症、前置胎盤、HELLP綜合症、緊急剖腹產等）。而 AXA 則完全不保所有產科情況及其併發症。考慮到約8至15%的懷孕個案會出現某種形式的併發症，僅此一項保障已可節省數萬甚至數十萬港元。

2. 更全面的基本保障

AIA 在所有計劃級別均涵蓋中醫門診、第三期臨床試驗藥物、緊急牙科及私人護理，並非只限頂級計劃。

3. 更長的術後覆診期

AIA 大手術後的門診覆診期最長365天，而 AXA 僅為180天。

4. 終身保證續保

AIA 終身保證續保，無年齡上限，而 AXA 則以100歲為限。

AXA 的優勝之處

AXA 無終身保障上限、基本保費較低、折扣計劃更優厚（最多35%），且即時保障未知的已有病症。若懷孕並非考慮因素，AXA 極具競爭力。

💡 行動建議

  1. 立即申請 AIA 優越保 Ultra——懷孕併發症300天等候期由保單生效日起計，時間至關重要。
  2. 選擇亞洲地區保障，除非您計劃在亞洲以外地區分娩。
  3. 考慮選擇25,000港元墊底費選項（年繳保費約7,664港元），在保費節省與自付費用之間取得平衡。
  4. 加入 AIA Vitality，在第1年即享10%保費折扣。
  5. 根據您選擇的墊底費，每年預算至少7,000至19,000港元。
