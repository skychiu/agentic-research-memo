VHIS Plan Comparison 自願醫保計劃比較

For a 35-year-old woman planning to start a family 適用於計劃生育的35歲女性

📋 Executive Summary 執行摘要

We compared five VHIS Flexi Plans — AIA Privilege Ultra, AXA WiseGuard Pro, HSBC VHIS Flexi, Manulife First VHIS, and Prudential PRUHealth VHIS VIP — across premiums, benefit coverage, pregnancy-related protection, and value for a 35-year-old woman planning a family.

Our top pick is AIA Privilege Ultra (Asia coverage, $0 deductible) at HKD 19,288/year. It offers full cover on room & board, surgeon's fees (regardless of surgical category), cancer treatment, and diagnostic imaging — with no sub-limits on core hospitalisation items. Crucially, it is one of only two plans that explicitly names pregnancy complications as a covered benefit (fully covered, 300-day waiting period) in its benefit schedule.

Prudential PRUHealth VHIS VIP is the close runner-up — nearly identical full-cover structure, explicit pregnancy complications coverage, and slightly cheaper at HKD 17,554/year, though its lifetime limit (HKD 56M) is modestly lower than AIA's (HKD 60M).

Manulife First VHIS offers the lowest premiums (HKD 4,354–7,735/year for women), but imposes per-item sub-limits that will leave significant out-of-pocket gaps at private hospitals. It does not explicitly highlight pregnancy complications in its brochure, and its geographic coverage is Hong Kong only.

We recommend $0 deductible for this profile. A higher deductible can halve the premium, but a woman planning a family faces near-certain hospitalisation within the next few years. Each claim year she would absorb HKD 16,000–50,000 out of pocket before insurance pays — wiping out years of premium savings in a single event.

💰 Why We Recommend $0 Deductible 為何建議選擇$0自付費

A deductible is the amount you pay out of pocket each policy year before your insurer begins to reimburse. Choosing a higher deductible lowers your premium — but for a woman planning a family, the maths strongly favours $0.

Why $0 Is Right for This Profile

  1. Near-certain hospitalisation in 1–3 years. With AIA's $16K deductible, she saves $11,368/yr in premium but pays $16,000 out of pocket the moment she's admitted — a net loss of $4,632 in the claim year. AIA Premium Table page_001, page_005
  2. Deductible resets every policy year. Complications in one year + delivery the next = paying the deductible twice. Two claims with $16K deductible = $32,000 out of pocket, exceeding $22,736 in two years' premium savings.
  3. $0 deductible = first-dollar coverage for pregnancy complications. Every dollar of eligible expenses is reimbursed immediately — no financial barrier to seeking timely care. AIA Product brochure page_016
  4. Higher deductibles suit low-claim-risk profiles. A healthy 25-year-old with no hospitalisation plans might rationally choose $50K deductible. A 35-year-old planning a family is entering the highest-utilisation period of her insurance life.
💡 Exception: If you already have a company group medical plan covering the first $16K–50K, a higher-deductible VHIS plan becomes an excellent cost-effective top-up. In that case, Prudential VIP at $6,574/yr ($20K ded) or AIA at $7,920/yr ($16K ded) offer superb value — still with explicit pregnancy complications coverage and full cover above the deductible.

為何$0自付費適合此情況

  1. 1–3年內幾乎必定住院。以友邦$16K自付費為例，每年省 $11,368保費，但一入院即需自付 $16,000 — 索償年度淨損失 $4,632。友邦保費表 page_001, page_005
  2. 自付費每年重設。若一年出現併發症、翌年分娩住院 = 需支付自付費兩次。兩年$16K自付費 = $32,000，超過兩年保費節省的 $22,736。
  3. $0自付費 = 首元保障懷孕併發症。每一元合資格費用均即時獲賠 — 求醫無財務障礙。友邦產品小冊子 page_016
  4. 較高自付費適合低索償風險人士。無住院計劃的25歲健康人士可合理選擇$50K自付費。計劃生育的35歲女性正進入醫療保險使用率最高的時期。
💡 例外情況：如你已有公司團體醫療保險可承擔首$16K–50K住院費用，較高自付費的自願醫保可作為高性價比的補充保障。此情況下，保誠尚賓年繳 $6,574（$20K自付費）或友邦年繳 $7,920（$16K自付費）均屬優良選擇 — 仍享有明確的懷孕併發症保障及自付費以上的全額保障。

⏰ Practical Tip for Family Planning 生育規劃實用建議

Buy early. The 300-day waiting period for pregnancy complications means you should secure your policy at least 10 months before planned conception. If you are already pregnant, the pregnancy complications benefit will not apply to this pregnancy regardless of which plan you choose.

盡早投保。懷孕併發症設有300天等候期，意味你應在計劃懷孕前至少10個月投保。如你已經懷孕，無論選擇哪個計劃，懷孕併發症保障均不適用於本次懷孕。
